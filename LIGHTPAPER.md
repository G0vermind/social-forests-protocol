## 1. Abstract

Social Forest is a decentralized Regenerative Finance (ReFi) protocol built on the Stellar network. It engineers a trustless bridge between corporate ESG capital and verified physical silviculture. By utilizing an Autonomous AI Oracle to process real-world biomass data and trigger Soroban smart contracts, Social Forest tokenizes African Mahogany (*Khaya senegalensis*) into programmable, yield-bearing Real-World Assets (RWAs).

---

## 2. The Problem: The ESG Friction

The current voluntary carbon and ESG market is fragmented, slow, and opaque.

- **Auditing Bottlenecks:** Traditional forestry auditing is manual and prohibitively expensive.
- **Lack of Liquidity:** Environmental assets are often illiquid.
- **Greenwashing:** Corporate capital struggles to find verifiable projects.

---

## 3. The Protocol Solution

Social Forest resolves these inefficiencies through a dual-layer architecture.

### 3.1. The Physical Layer (Client Zero)

The protocol is fully collateralized by an operational supply chain in Ceará, Brazil.

- **Cultivation:** Seedling biometric tracking originates at Viveiro Maravilha.
- **Processing:** The economic cycle concludes at the Sómogno sawmill.
- **Regulatory Moat:** African Mahogany is exempt from the Document of Forest Origin (DOF), ensuring high-speed asset liquidity.

### 3.2. The Web3 Layer (Soroban & AI Oracle)

- **Proof of Growth (PoG):** An autonomous AI Oracle digests raw field data.
- **x402 Data Monetization:** Auditors access this verified on-chain data via Stellar's x402 micropayment standard.
- **Soroban Smart Contracts:** Verified growth data triggers automated minting of environmental assets.

---

## 4. B2B2C Economic Engine & Tokenomics

- **B2B Utility:** Corporations utilize fiat-on-ramps (Stripe integration) to purchase tokenized forestry assets, neutralizing Carbon Debt (C-DEBT).
- **B2C Engagement:** End consumers receive fractionalized digital assets ('Leaves').
