# Social Forest 🌳
**A Decentralized ReFi & RWA Protocol on the Stellar Network**

Social Forest is an open-source Regenerative Finance (ReFi) protocol that bridges the gap between high-impact forestry and the Web3 ecosystem. By transforming living trees into autonomous digital agents via Soroban smart contracts and x402 machine-to-machine (M2M) micropayments, we provide a gamified B2B2C ecosystem backed by transparent, on-chain climate auditing.

## 🌍 The "Client Zero" & Physical Infrastructure

Unlike purely theoretical Web3 projects, Social Forest is backed by fully operational physical infrastructure. The protocol's Genesis Phase is validated and collateralized by the **African Mahogany (*Khaya senegalensis*)** supply chain based in **Nova Russas, Ceará (Brazil)**. 

This vertical integration includes:
* **Viveiro Maravilha:** The nursery where the physical seedlings are cultivated and their initial biometric data is captured.
* **Sómogno:** The sawmill that guarantees the final processing and economic output of the timber, completing the asset's lifecycle.

## ⚙️ Core Architecture

The protocol is divided into three main pillars:

### 1. B2B2C Gamification & RWA Tokenization
Consumers "mine" digital fractions of real trees (Leaves) by purchasing products or completing tasks from partner brands. These micro-assets are distributed frictionlessly using the **Stellar x402 micropayment standard**, allowing users to forge their digital trees in a mobile-first "Digital Nursery" interface.

### 2. On-Chain Climate Auditing (C-CRED & C-DEBT)
An automated clearinghouse for corporate ESG compliance. Real-world biomass growth generates Carbon Credits (**C-CRED**), which are used to neutralize Corporate Carbon Debts (**C-DEBT**). This issues cryptographic proof of alignment with Net Zero targets and UN Sustainable Development Goals (**SDGs 8, 9, 12, 13, and 15**).

### 3. Proof of Growth (The Physical Oracle)
Integration with automated workflows (n8n) acts as a physical oracle, bridging real-world biometric data (DBH and height) directly into the Supabase backend, triggering smart contracts on Stellar to update asset value based on actual forestry growth.

## 🏛️ Decentralized Governance: Social Forest DAO

To ensure global scalability, transparency, and community management, the protocol operates under a Decentralized Autonomous Organization structure, powered by the **$FLORA** governance token.

* **RWA Onboarding:** Token holders act as decentralized auditors, voting on the admission of new global forestry projects based on technical rigor and SDG alignment.
* **Community Treasury:** A micro-fee from C-DEBT liquidations funds the DAO treasury, which finances cutting-edge sustainable agritech and retail adoption programs.
* **Progressive Decentralization:** Initially guided by the Core Team and Strategic Board to ensure agile Soroban/x402 deployment during the B2B validation sprints, governance and TVL control will systematically transition to the community.

## 🌱 Scalability: An Asset-Agnostic Future

While the Genesis Phase focuses on African Mahogany due to our proprietary vertical infrastructure in Ceará, the Soroban smart contracts are designed to be **strictly asset-agnostic**.

Social Forest will scale to become the global standard for tokenizing diverse natural assets, including:
* High-yield crops (Rubber trees, Macaúba, Eucalyptus).
* Agroforestry Systems focusing on food security (Cocoa, Açaí, Brazil nuts).
* Standing native forests dedicated exclusively to conservation and biodiversity credits.
